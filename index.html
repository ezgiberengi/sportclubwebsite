We are not just a sport club,
we are family.

The philosophy of the Club is to bet on male and female coaches helping the little ones with their training so that they become part of our main project. The training of the little ones is the key piece to later obtain players with a high technical and tactical level.

The Pòrtol Volleyball Club is a Mallorcan club founded in 1993. The men's team was promoted in the 1997/1998 season in the First National Division and in the following season (1998/1999) they were promoted to the FEV League.

During the 1999/2000 season, they played as second classified in the regular league, the final phase of promotion to the Honor Division or Super League, the highest category of national volleyball. The 2000/2001 season, he was fighting among the top positions in the classification, contesting as second classified in the regular league the final phase of promotion to the Honor Division, finally achieving promotion to the highest category of national volleyball.

The season, 2001/2002, the club managed to maintain the category being classified in the tenth position of a total of 14 teams. In the 2002/2003 season, he improved the ranking to seventh place and the club already had some internationally renowned figures.

In 2003 Son Amar joined the project as the first sponsor. Son Amar was once the most important club in Spain, achieving:

Six titles of champion of the Spanish League.

Five championships of the Copa del Rey

Three European finals

Fifth place in the Brazil Club World Cup

The prestige that Palma de Mallorca had at the international volleyball level was a recovery during the 2003/04 and 2004/05 seasons. In the first of these seasons, the club build a team to fight to be champion of the League and the King's Cup. Neither of these two Goals WAS possible but, without a doubt, the foundations of one of the most powerful clubs on the continent were established in terms of sporting potential.

To the aforementioned titles must be added those obtained in the 2004/05 season with the number of Volleyball Pòrtol / Son Amar Palma. This last year the club has been:

Runner-up of the CEV Cup 2004/05

Runner-up of the Superliga 2004/05

Champion of the Copa del Rey 2004/05

All the categories are obtaining great results both at the regional level and with the different participations in the Spanish Championships. At the beginning of this year, a specialized digital platform within national volleyball (TODOVOLEY) ranked us fourth out of a total of 131 clubs nationwide. Therefore, they are facts that make us feel proud of the great work done.

We keep working and updating ourselves at the same time in order to be at the forefront of national volleyball!
